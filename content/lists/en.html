To join any list you need to register by using its web UI, or by using [email command](#mailreg) or by [using this quick form](#fastoption). When you're registered to a list you can send and receive email to/from all members of the list. When sending new message to a list you need to [follow some common rules](#rules). If you just want to read lists' message without being a member, you may use the `rss` links as provided below. ## Lists {#all} * **theslinux-`security`@freelists.org** : Focus on seucirty topics. [Web-UI][security.web], [archive][security.archive], [RSS feed][security.rss], [Registration form](#fastoption). * **theslinux-`questions`@freelists.org** : General questions from users. [Web-UI][questions.web], [archive][questions.archive], [RSS feed][questions.rss], [Registration form](#fastoption). * **theslinux-`bugs`@freelists.org** : Reporting bugs. You make use the [Github system][github.issues] instead. [Web-UI][bugs.web], [archive][bugs.archive], [RSS feed][bugs.rss], [Registration form](#fastoption). * **theslinux-`hackers`@freelists.org** : This is for hackers and developers. [Web-UI][hackers.web], [archive][hackers.archive], [RSS][hackers.rss], [Registration form](#fastoption). ## Common rules {#rules} Our lists are public. Once your email is sent to the list, any one who has internet connection can receive and read your message. So the first rule for you is to `be careful`, and `to respect other people`. Every list has its own kind of topics. Don't use wrong list for your problem. In case you are not sure, the list `theslinux-questions` may be the good one. You can use English or Vietnamese in our lists. However, please don't use both English and Vietnamese in a **same** topic (thread). If you think your problem or message doesn't follow up the current thread, you may edit the email subject and/or use the prefix `[OT]` to help other people to track discussion. ## Email command {#emailreg} To register to the list `foobar@freelists.org`, you can send an email to the address `foobar-request@freelists.org` (here `foobar` is `theslinux-questions`, `theslinux-hackers`, bla bla), and use **subscribe** as email subject. You will need to wait some minutes after your email is sent out. The list server will answer you with the last steps to finish your registration. Our lists are run by `Ecartis` which also provides many useful email commands. Please read `Ecartis` documentation for details. ## Quick registration form {#fastoption} You can register and/or change your list' setting by using the following form. ---- {::nomarkdown}
